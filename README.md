## 👩‍💻 About Me

I am an undergraduate **Computer Science and Engineering** student at the **University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka**, passionate about software engineering, artificial intelligence, and building technology that solves real-world problems.

My journey in computing has led me to explore different areas of software development, including machine learning, backend development, databases, computer architecture, and systems programming. I enjoy understanding how things work internally — from low-level concepts such as processors, memory, and operating systems to high-level applications powered by AI and modern web technologies.

Currently, I am working on expanding my knowledge in areas such as artificial intelligence, full-stack development, cloud technologies, DevOps practices, and software quality engineering. I enjoy experimenting with new technologies, learning through practical projects, and continuously improving my problem-solving skills.

### 🧠 AI & Engineering
I have hands-on experience developing projects using Python, Java, C++, JavaScript, and various frameworks and tools. I have worked on machine learning projects involving data processing, model training, and deployment, including handwritten digit recognition systems and interactive applications. I am also interested in AI-driven software engineering solutions, such as using machine learning to analyze, improve, and maintain software systems.

### 🎨 Creativity & Design
Beyond programming, I have a strong interest in creativity and design. Drawing, singing, and exploring new ideas help me approach technical challenges from different perspectives. I believe good software is not only about writing code but also about understanding users, designing meaningful solutions, and creating positive experiences.

> **My goal is to become a versatile software engineer who can combine strong technical foundations with creativity to build reliable, intelligent, and impactful systems.**
